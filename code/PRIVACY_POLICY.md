# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 22, 2025**

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how SoulHug collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Conversation Data:** We process the text you input to generate responses.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect anonymous metrics (e.g., session length, feature usage) to improve the Service.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide the AI chat service.
- To maintain "memory" within a session (e.g., remembering your parent's personality traits during a roleplay).
- To improve the quality of the AI models (anonymized data only).

## 3. Data Storage

- **Local Storage:** Some preferences may be stored locally on your device.
- **Server Processing:** Your messages are sent to our servers for processing by the AI model. We do not permanently store your personal conversations linked to your real identity.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party AI providers (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI) to process your messages. These providers adhere to strict data privacy standards but may retain data for short periods for abuse monitoring.

## 5. Children's Privacy

SoulHug is designed for teenagers and young adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@soulhug.app.
